GigMan Want Ads is an industry-specific tool for advertising goods and services. It is currently under construction, but is fully functional.
There are seven main categories under which to create advertisements:

Business: Business related services, such as accounting.

Management: Management services, such as tour or stage management.

Musicians: Musicians for hire or seeking work,.

Technicians: Technicians for hire or seeking work.

Gigs: Venus seeking artists, or artists seeking gigs.

Instruments: Musical instruments

Gear: Musical or production gear not covered under Instruments



Ads can be created by anyone with a GigMan account. Media, such as audio, images or YouTube video can be added to enhance an ads appeal.
