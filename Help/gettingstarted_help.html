There are a few basic steps you must take to get the greatest benefit from GigMan. These steps are outlined below.

  1. Create a user account, or log into an existing account.
    Wheen you first arrive at the site, you are redirected to the landing page, where you can choose to create an account or log in.
  2. Create a new Artist, or send a join request to an existing artist.
    Once you are logged in, you can choose to create a new artist or join an existing artist account.
    If you create an artist, you are automatically affiliated with the Artist accont, and can accept join requests from other GigMan members.
    Alternately, you can, in your profile editor, choose an existing Artist from the drop down at the bottom of the page and send a request to join it.
  3. Start creating a song list.
    From the navigation panel, or from the main page, click the 'Add Song' tile. The song editor will open, and allow you to start adding songs to your library.
    When you create an artist, a sample song is added to your account. This is simply to illustrate the song list and lyric page, and can be deleted.
    To get a sense of the song list dynamic, from the navigation panel or the main page, click the 'Song List' tile. You should see one entry in the list - 'Sample Song'. Click the title to go to the lyric view page.
  4. Become a premium artist.
    While in test mode, premium features are free, and testers who report valid bugs get a year of premium membership (for one artist account) free after launch. All testers will get a year of GigMan Request for free after launch.
    To make your artist account a Premium account, or to make it a GigMan Request member, go through the payment process, accessible from the Artist Profile editor. No payment need be made.
    Becoming a Premium member will allow you to save your song list to the database, and gives you access to a calendar. If you are also a GigMan Request me mber, the calendar can be made public so your audience can see upcoming events.
    Becoming a GigMan Request member will allow your audience to make requests and message the artist in near real-time, and allow the audience to see public calendar events.
