GigMan Request allows stage artists to receive requests and messages from their audience in near-real time, and view them on-stage over their wireless devices.

The performing artist can direct their audience members to use their smart phones to go to gigman.com/xxx, where xxx is an identifier the artidst can define.

Once there, the audience member can make requests based on a list of songs defined by the artist, and send messages to the individual stage artists, leader or entire band.
Messages are limited to 160 characters, and can be prefaced with a number of menu selected topics.

Premium GigMan artists can also publish event dates to their GigMan Request page, so audience members can see where upcoming gigs are occurring.

See GigMan Request | Artist Preferences for a summary of how the artist can control audience input.

Messages and requests can be viewd in the navigation popout (). An analytical view is also available in the Dashboard section of the site.
