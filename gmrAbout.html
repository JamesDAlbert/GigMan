GigMan Request lets fans and bands interact in real time while the performance is happening.

If you are an artist, you can create a song list that your audience can browse and upvote to make requests, which are made available to you in real time if you are using our free GigMan software.

If you are an audience member, GigMan Request allows you to ask those folks on the stage to play your favorite songs from their repertoire.

You can make requests, each of which can be an up-vote () or, if available, a down-vote ().

Requests are tallied in real-time and delivered to the artists' wireless devices.
